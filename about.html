About Me: Rachel Navarrette

Why computer science?

I have always been fascinated by technology. I feel that given my age, I have been lucky to experience how fast technology has changed over the years. From watching movies via VHS tapes to using touch screens on a cell phone and tablet, to experiencing virtual and augmented reality, technology has impacted and improved our life. I enjoy learning and using programming languages as a vessel to solve problems. It amazes me how the computer programs work so efficiently to solve equations or perform jobs. My current computer science interests include machine-learning, artificial intelligence, security, and Augmented/Virtual reality.
